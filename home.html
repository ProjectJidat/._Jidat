Project Jidat | جدت: Empowering Seniors, Spreading Knowledge

Our Aim:

  1. Empowering Seniors with Gadget Literacy
  2. Spreading Knowledge about Robotics
  3. Spreading Knowledge about AI
  4. Spreading Knowledge about STEM Education
Gadget Literacy:
  • Introduce seniors to the basics of smartphones, tablets, and computers
  • Encourage digital inclusion and confidence
Robotics Knowledge:
  • Educate about the fundamentals of robotics and its applications
  • Inspire interest in robotics and its potential
AI Knowledge:
  • Explain the basics of artificial intelligence and its impact
  • Explore AI's potential and ethical considerations
STEM Education:
  • Promote the importance of STEM education for all ages
  • Encourage lifelong learning and exploration
